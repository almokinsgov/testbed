Welcome To The Budget Priority Builder

This tool lets you explore how different budget choices may affect Council services and likely public reaction. Choose how you would like to begin, then adjust the sliders at any time.

You can change your budget at any time after this by moving the sliders, or by using the quick actions at the top of the page.
Before You Start
  • The public reaction scores are a guide to help explain possible community responses. They are not a prediction or a final measure of public opinion.
  • Your results help show budget priorities and trade-offs. They are part of the engagement process and do not make a final Council decision.
  • Please only include information you are comfortable sharing. Council should confirm how responses will be stored, used and reported before public release.
